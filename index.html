Dendebar

Significant Characters in Dendebar

Thordo Dormah / Asair: An Ancient Gold Dragon, born 2766 years ago, and currently the King of Dendebar. Throdo lives as a human.

Ak’ros: Blue dragonborn living in Dendebar, and the resident blacksmith of Dendebar.

Kuku: A kenku researching the mines in Dendebar and the ancient language located on the runes inside the mines, which showed connections to the Nine Hells.

Isaiah: Left hand of Thordo Dormah, Head of University of Dendebar and lead scientist of the city.

Culrian: One of the defenders of Arvandor, left in Terrian after the Great War by greater deities as protector of the mortal realm. Buried deep in the mines of Dendebar, alongside his longsword, Custrios, later wielded by Ko’roth Bloodscale.

Varentis

Meeting ground of the Goliaths from across the North of Valkari. Once every year, the Goliaths from the Greystone, Icepeak, and Frostridge tribes meet here to exchange goods and culture. The meeting ground is situated around a large tree, the burial ground of Frost Giants for centuries, causing conflict between Goliaths and Giants.

Key Events

After the arrival of the 7 Sons, who sought discourse with Goliath leaders and Xador, Father of the Giants, an agreement was reached between Goliaths and Giants, who combined forces to join the rebellion.

Whilst in Varentis, Irarok fought the previous chieftain of the Greystone tribe and became the new chieftain.

Blackvale Pass

The location of a large-scale battle between the Empire forces and the rebellion led by the 7 Sons of Valkari. Empire led by Acrobrahn Bevrash with Navare and Arannis of the Vigilun.

Significant Characters in the Blackvale Pass

Baki: Child of the Blue dragonborn tribe in ghostly form. Died with tribe buried nearby, protected by sentient trees.

Willmo Eldaron “Blade”: Younger brother of Minato Eldaron. Former assassin, killed by the party and later resurrected. Remembers nothing before meeting the Queen of Nemar.

Navare: High-Elf Vigilun archer.

Arannis: Mage of the Empire who resurrected the blue dragon Alkeon beneath Lorenede along with blue dragonborn warriors.

Blackvale

An Empire-controlled mining village where slaves mine Blackvale Mountain for a Gem of Al’Garad.
